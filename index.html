About me

I am a painter from China who has studied in Italy for five years and traveled to various European countries, gaining a profound understanding and love for European culture and tourism. My expertise lies in cultural heritage and oil painting, and I have extensive experience in creative work with outstanding pieces. I am also passionate about photography, capturing beautiful moments in life through my lens.

In my creative work, I focus on inheriting and promoting Chinese culture while also being deeply influenced by European culture, blending the two in my artwork. I believe that culture is the spiritual wealth of humanity, a treasure we should all strive to inherit and protect.

I am looking forward to exchanging ideas and collaborating with fellow scholars on the creation of cultural heritage and oil painting, sharing our experiences and insights, and contributing to the development of cultural endeavors. Let us work together towards the prosperity of cultural development!

Skills

Excellent knowledge of the main raster and vector graphics design software (Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, In Design) and digital illustration software (Procreate).

I have over 10 years of experience in the field of drawing, with knowledge of software such as Procreate and Adobe Photoshop. I am experienced in the description and reconstruction of visual scenes of heritage sites in various styles, particularly in the restoration of European heritage landscapes. I am able to design and draw specific scenes in different historical periods, based on textual information. I have participated in three archaeological projects (Castelseprio, Monselice, Monte Baldo), to reconstruct different landscape features in various historical
periods, by using Procreate and the graphics tablet.

Some of my works have won international prizes and have been accepted by exhibitions in China.
